Plant, Set & Forget — Hawaiʻi Zone Finder

A public food-sovereignty resource. Enter your Hawaiʻi town or area to find your planting zone and what will thrive — ʻulu (breadfruit) and kalo (taro) — with cited guidance from Tam Hunt's PS&F zone guides.

Covers clearly-zoned places on Hawaiʻi Island, Maui, and Oʻahu.

Guidance drawn from the Plant, Set & Forget zone guides by Tam Hunt, part of the Hawaiʻi Island Food Security Initiative (Think B.I.G. Hawaiʻi and The Food Basket). Additional reference: CTAHR Cooperative Extension Home Garden Vegetable: Upland Taro (HGV-18, Oct. 1998). Every claim on this page traces to one of those sources.
